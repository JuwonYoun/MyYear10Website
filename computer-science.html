Evaluation

While learning computer science, I came to clearly understand the conversion methods of binary, decimal, and hexadecimal.
Since computers operate based on binary, I learned that they process all data as a combination of 0 and 1.
I learned that hexadecimal is effective in expressing a lot of data concisely, such as memory addresses or color expressions.
Although decimal is familiar to humans, I also learned that it is disadvantageous in terms of efficiency because it is converted to binary and processed inside the computer.
In conclusion, I realized that understanding the number system is essential to deeply understanding the operating principles of computers and how data is expressed.
